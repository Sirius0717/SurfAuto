About Surf Auto

Changzhou Surf Automobile Sales Co., Ltd. is a professional Chinese auto export company dedicated to connecting global buyers with China's finest vehicles and auto parts. With years of hands-on experience, our team has earned a reputation for reliability, precision, and integrity in every transaction.

We deliver complete one-stop export solutions — vehicle sourcing, competitive pricing, customs clearance, international logistics, and final delivery. Our clients across South America, Central Asia, and the Middle East trust us as their long-term partner for Chinese automotive products.
